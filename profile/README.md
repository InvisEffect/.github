Welcome to InvisEffect, the best platform to bypass restrictions and content filtering

### About InvisEffect
InvisEffect is a passion project, passed down by a friend. InvisEffect's goal is to allow the user to keep school or work admistration
away from this site, while being extremely customizable and have tons of games to play.

### InvisEffect v. Competitors
InvisEffect runs off of Amazon AWS servers, which means that your school or workplace already have them whitelisted, compared
to others with custom links or GitHub. This ensures that you can safely game without having to worry about the
site being blocked by your school or work admistration.

As opposed to other sites, InvisEffect is fully open-source, which means you can see what is going on within the code of the
website. This can build trust with out community and allow others to help build up InvisEffect.

### What We Offer
InvisEffect offers tons of unblocked games by user requests or from trends on the internet. We also offer a complete proxy,
allowing you to search anything on the world-wide-web, without it being saved to your history. Users can also change how the
disguise looks of their tab, and cloak it with an `about:blank` tab.

There are many more coming soon to InvisEffect, so be on the look out for new features.
